Recommended cycle

  1. Read one numbered lesson and watch its assigned videos.
  2. Use the exam-focus panel to separate easily confused concepts.
  3. Run the objective drill linked from that lesson.
  4. Complete a related lab or explain the concept aloud from memory.
  5. Use full practice exams to identify the next weak objective.
